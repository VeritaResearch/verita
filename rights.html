Terms, Conditions, and Privacy Policy

Verita Research is an independent research group. Please read these terms before submitting answers to any of our polls. By submitting answers, you are agreeing to the terms.

We may change these terms at any time, and we will do our best to notify you when we do so.

Our polling is not directed at children. Use of our polls is only for those over the age of 13 (or 16 in the European Union).

We promise to treat your data ethically. We believe in, and commit to honoring, the right to be forgotten: at any point in time, you may contact us to have your data permanently deleted from our records.

All personally identifiable information (e.g. email addresses) will never, under any circumstances, be released publicly.

Any other type of data released, either publicly or to third-parties, will be done using differential privacy. You may contact us at any time for more information regarding our methodology for doing so.

Aggregated results of survey data will be publicly released (e.g. X% of Group Y support Candidate Z), and may be released to third parties, including as the product of statistical models based on the survey data.

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will do our best to notify you.
