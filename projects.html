Projects
Election polling for online streaming audiences
The primary goal of this project is to analyze the political affiliations and perspectives of audiences who follow popular political streamers on streaming platforms like Twitch, YouTube, and Kick, with a specific focus on their views regarding the 2024 U.S. presidential election.
Automatic fact-checking software
This project aims to use machine learning to create an automated fact-checking and source-finding system. The system will be designed to analyze statements and quickly verify their veracity against a database of credible sources. To keep up to date with the project, sign up for our newsletter.
